Air Terjun Nglirip

Nglirip Waterfall is located in Mulyo Agung Village, precisely in Jojogan Hamlet, Singgahan District. The distance between these villages from downtown Tuban is about 37 km from the Southwest. Its location, which is not too far from the city center of Tuban, is also very easy to reach by using private vehicles or by using public transportation. Nglirip Waterfall is very familiarly known as Grajagan Nglirip. This tourist attraction is indeed a very exotic prima donna on the Ronggolawe Earth. The condition of this waterfall is still very natural and the trees that grow around it are very thick and big.
