Gunung Bromo

Mount Bromo (from Sanskrit: Brahma, one of the Main Gods in Hinduism) or in the Tengger language spelled "Brama" , is an active volcano in East Java, Indonesia. This mountain has an altitude of 2,329 meters above sea level and is located in four districts, namely Probolinggo Regency, Pasuruan Regency, Lumajang Regency, and Malang Regency. Mount Bromo is famous as a major tourist attraction in East Java. As a tourist attraction, Bromo is attractive because of its status as an active volcano. Mount Bromo is included in the Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park area.
